Registration Fee

How are fees levied?

Registration fees are only collected from teams invited to the IBCOL finals. Payment details are provided with the invitation letter. Fee depends on country income level:

  • Category A: High income.
  • Category B: Upper middle income.
  • Category C: Low and lower middle income.

Why are fees levied?

The fees contribute to costs including logistics, food, beverages, and posterboard printing. No profits are extracted from these fees.
